Simple Event Logic
09 / 14

How we separate pickup and putdown

Pickup

  • Hand moves toward shelf.
  • Item leaves shelf.
  • Hand carries item away.

Putdown

  • Hand brings item to shelf.
  • Item appears on shelf.
  • Hand leaves without item.
Direction of change defines event type
